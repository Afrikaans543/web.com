¿Que es el PET?

a common type of plastic used in beverage containers and other products, should
go to the plastics recycling section, usually marked with the number 1 or 2.

PET is polyethylene terephthalate, a material commonly used in the manufacture
packaging. PET is a linear polymer and for this reason it can be modified after
several processes, which is why it is important in recycling. PET is a safe
material and contains no harmful chemicals. This means it doesn't release
toxins or harmful substances into the environment when recycled or decomposed..

WHAT IS IT FOR?

Because it allows materials to be recycled and reused,
reducing pollution and conserving natural resources.

Bottles, containers, caps...
Anything made with it can end
up becoming a new object with a totally different function.
We reduce the need to produce new PET, which in turn reduces
consumption of natural resources and greenhouse gas emissions.
